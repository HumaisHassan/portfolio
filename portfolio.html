HARVEY'S

I had the privilege of crafting a bespoke website for Harvey's, a burgeoning startup owned by my dear friend Ahsan, who shares my pursuit of a BSCS degree at BNU. Recognizing the potential to elevate his business through a digital presence, I tailored the website to seamlessly showcase Harvey's offerings. Specializing in a delectable array of burgers, wraps, and sandwiches, Harvey's culinary delights now enjoy an online spotlight. It's a testament to the fusion of technology and entrepreneurship, and I'm proud to contribute to Ahsan's journey in the vibrant world of food and technology.
